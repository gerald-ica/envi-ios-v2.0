<objective>
Refactor the 3 views that violate the repo-in-view anti-pattern: `SystemHealthView`, `SSOConfigView`, `ContractManagerView`. Each gets a real ViewModel and stops injecting a repository directly on the view struct.

Purpose: These 3 views currently hold `private let repo: SomeRepository` as a view property. That breaks testability (can't inject a mock without subclassing a View) and makes loading/error state handling ad-hoc. Every other feature group in the app uses a ViewModel; these are the outliers.

Output: `SystemHealthViewModel`, `SSOConfigViewModel`, `ContractManagerViewModel` â€” each owning the repo interaction. Views consume the VMs. No behavior changes, just shape.
</objective>
