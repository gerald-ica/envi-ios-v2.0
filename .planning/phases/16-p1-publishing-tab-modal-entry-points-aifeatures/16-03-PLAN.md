- ChatExploreView uses `.requiresAura()` — AI tools are premium. The new menu should inherit that gating automatically (it's already applied at the tab level).
- The 7 AI features should feel like a *toolbelt*, not a separate tab. Grid of 2 columns × 4 rows (1 empty) is sensible; or list with icons + names + short descriptions.
- WorldExplorer is UIKit-bridged via `HelixSceneController`. Don't touch WorldExplorer; put the AI tools menu inside the ChatExploreView mode-switcher, not as a WorldExplorer addition.
